## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement macOS Simplified Chinese localization for the menu bar app/settings UI: the relevant labels are still hardcoded English strings, the macOS app target does not ship localization resources, and the extra voice-wake note in the report appears to be a separate platform gate rather than evidence that localization landed.
