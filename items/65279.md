## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` contains a MiniMax-specific mitigation that disables implicit thinking on Anthropic-compatible MiniMax requests, but the code/docs/test evidence only proves reasoning-payload handling, not that the reported 10+ second MiniMax CN first-response delay is fixed. The issue is recent and still plausible, and I do not have a reproduced current-`main` non-repro to justify closure.
