## Summary

Current `main` already carries `delivery.threadId` through cron announce delivery for Telegram topics, and the changelog records the matching fix in release `2026.3.28`.

## Evidence

  - file: [CHANGELOG.md:1937](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/28fc03c386e14db502429bbe7e062cbe90439ca1/CHANGELOG.md#L1937)

  - command: `nl -ba src/cron/delivery-plan.ts | sed -n '44,68p'`
