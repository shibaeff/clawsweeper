## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still requires an add-on principal binding for Google Chat add-on JWTs, and the shipped Google Chat docs/config examples do not explain that `channels.googlechat.appPrincipal` must match the JWT `sub` value. The reporter’s follow-up says the 401 stopped only after switching `appPrincipal` from the service-account email to the numeric principal from the token, so this reads as a real docs/config gap rather than a conclusively obsolete report.

## Evidence

  - file: [extensions/googlechat/src/monitor-webhook.ts:108](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/11ad1919edc7278f9d3e36c87c56bd3cb52c6e41/extensions/googlechat/src/monitor-webhook.ts#L108)
