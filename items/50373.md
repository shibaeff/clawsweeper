## Summary

Issue #50373 is already implemented on current `main` and in the shipped `v2026.4.22` release: cron delivery now normalizes id-like targets before announce delivery, and the Mattermost plugin resolves opaque 26-character IDs to `user:<id>` when they belong to a DM user.
