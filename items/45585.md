## Summary

Current `main` now routes compaction through transcript sanitization/validation, drops non-latest thinking blocks when policy requires it, and for Claude 4.x preserves signed Anthropic thinking immutably instead of mutating it. The issue appears implemented on `main`, but I could not tie it to a shipped release tag.
