## Summary

Current `main` already supports disabling built-in heartbeats via `agents.defaults.heartbeat.every: "0m"`. The exact `heartbeat: false` shape is not implemented, but the requested disable behavior is already documented, tested, and enforced by the scheduler/runtime.

## Evidence

  - file: [docs/gateway/config-agents.md:502](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/3de44fe593057b508d76f65abb36d58e0936ef1d/docs/gateway/config-agents.md#L502)
