## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still relies on the requester agent to rewrite completion/announce payloads into user-facing text, but the completion path continues to package raw child results plus session/runtime metadata into internal events, and I did not find a fail-closed outbound sanitizer or end-user delivery test that proves those operational details cannot leak into chat.
