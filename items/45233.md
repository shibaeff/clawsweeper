## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not show FreeBSD as a supported OpenClaw platform or release target. User-facing docs and installer paths enumerate macOS, Linux, and Windows/WSL2, while CI/release workflows are scoped to Linux, Windows, and macOS. There are a few internal FreeBSD compatibility checks in wrapper logic/tests, but they do not amount to the requested OS support, release packaging, or FreeBSD ports/pkg integration.
