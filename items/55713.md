## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still omits Mattermost from the reaction template/docs even though the Mattermost plugin advertises reaction support, so the PR is not stale or obsolete. The PR also is not a clear auto-close candidate because its review discussion identified at least one real doc mismatch with current behavior: Mattermost reaction removal still requires a non-empty `emoji`.
