## Summary

Current `main` already contains the Telegram long-poll recovery stack described in this issue: stalled `getUpdates` watchdog restarts, stale transport rebuild/disposal, HTTP/1-only Telegram dispatchers, transport-activity health publication, matching regression tests, and shipped changelog/docs coverage.

## Evidence
