## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still performs the startup persisted-offset `getUpdates(..., timeout: 0)` preflight that maintainers identified as the likely self-conflict path, and tests still assert that behavior. The shipped Telegram polling fixes in `v2026.4.23` harden transport recovery after 409s, but they do not remove the extra preflight call described in this issue.
