## Summary

Current `main` and the shipped `v2026.4.23` release already isolate Telegram forum topics as separate sessions by appending `:topic:<threadId>` to the group peer/session key. The report appears to conflate DM-only `session.dmScope` with Telegram group/topic routing.

## Evidence
