## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still reads full-resolution iOS picker images into memory, keeps the original bytes, builds previews from the raw data on the `@MainActor` chat view model, and base64-encodes attachments during send without any client-side resize/compression or total-budget enforcement. That matches the reported 48MP attachment failure/memory-pressure path rather than showing it was already fixed.

## Evidence

- **iOS chat composer loads raw picker bytes with no resize/compression step:** The iOS composer uses `PhotosPicker` and `loadTransferable(type: Data.self)`, then forwards the raw data directly into `addImageAttachment(...)`. There is no resizing, recompression, or byte-budgeting in this picker path.
