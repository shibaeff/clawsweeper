## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still hard-errors when `sessions_spawn` receives `streamTo: "parent"` with `runtime: "subagent"`, and the colocated unit test still asserts that error path. The PR remains actionable rather than stale or already implemented, and the discussion includes recent reproductions plus local validations of the proposed fix on shipped builds.
