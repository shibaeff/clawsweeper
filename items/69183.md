## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has fresh Apr 25 shared-media fixes for reply staging and root-threading, but this issue consolidates multiple surfaces and there is not high-confidence proof that the full regression cluster is resolved on `main`. The core allowlist guardrails remain, default media roots still do not include attachment-specific paths, and the reviewed tests/history do not show end-to-end coverage for the reported Telegram/WhatsApp/Control UI/webchat cases as a whole.
