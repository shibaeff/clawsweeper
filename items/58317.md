## Summary

Current `main` already implements the reset behavior through a newer, broader solution: `/new` and `/reset` clear auto-sourced fallback model/auth pins while preserving explicit user selections and spawned-session state, with coverage in both auto-reply and gateway tests. This supersedes the open PR’s unconditional clearing approach.
