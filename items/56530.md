## Summary

Current `main` still appears to have the reported Slack allowlist bug. `resolveSlackChannelConfig(...)` only builds match candidates from the bare channel ID, case variants, and optional channel-name forms, while the underlying matcher does exact-key lookup; a config entry like `channel:C0AJYR3BVTJ` would not match a runtime `channelId` of `C0AJYR3BVTJ`. The PR stays relevant and should remain open for maintainer review rather than auto-close.
