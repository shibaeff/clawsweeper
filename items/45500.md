## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still has a plausible duplicate-delivery path for this report: Discord-visible streamed replies are emitted before assistant-side failover handling, but the retry-safety metadata only treats messaging-tool sends, mutating tools, and cron adds as side effects. Assistant-side overloaded/rate-limit/internal-error handling can still rotate profiles or fall back and rerun after visible Discord output.
