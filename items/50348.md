## Summary

Current `main` already scopes Synology Chat inbound sessions by `accountId` and has regression coverage proving same-user messages on different accounts get different session keys, so the reported multi-account collapse is already fixed.

## Evidence

- **Scoped session-key helper on main:** `buildSynologyChatInboundSessionKey()` now delegates to `buildAgentSessionKey()` with `accountId` and `dmScope: "per-account-channel-peer"`, producing account-scoped DM keys instead of a flat `synology-chat-${user}` key.
