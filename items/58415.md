## Summary

Current main no longer routes `openai-codex/*` sessions into the native `ws-stream` path. Codex models are normalized onto the Codex HTTP transport (`openai-codex-responses` on the ChatGPT backend), and shipped regression tests now assert that `openai-codex` does not take the OpenAI websocket lane.
