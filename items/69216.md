## Summary

Current `main` no longer matches the reported behavior: `openclaw update` still delegates to `openclaw doctor --fix`, `openclaw.json` writes are protected by a rotating `.bak` backup ring, and the current doctor/update `.env` plumbing is read-only rather than rewriting `~/.openclaw/.env`.
