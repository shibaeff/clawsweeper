## Summary

Current `main` still configures the bundled Kimi Coding provider to use `api: "anthropic-messages"` at the built-in catalog and onboarding/config-preset layers, and the matching docs/tests still assert that behavior. The report is therefore not implemented away on `main`, and the issue should stay open.
