## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` already absorbed the Feishu portion through the newer generic configured-binding pipeline, but the PR's other advertised pieces are still missing on `main`: QQ Bot binding support is not present in the bundled plugin/docs, `acp.defaultChannels` does not exist in config/types/schema, catch-all ACP bindings are still rejected, and ACPX auto-enable still only keys off explicit ACP config. This means the PR is not implemented on `main`, even though part of it has been superseded and would need a substantial rebase onto the new binding architecture.
