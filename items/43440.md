## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still launches `imsg rpc` as a child process from the Node/OpenClaw process, and the shipped docs/setup text still state that Full Disk Access must be granted to the OpenClaw/`imsg` process context. That means the requested change to make `imsg` access `chat.db` solely in its own permission context is not implemented on `main` at `28fc03c386e14db502429bbe7e062cbe90439ca1`.

## Evidence
