## Summary

Current `main` already fixes the self-sustaining Telegram polling 409 loop by resetting polling state and forcing a fresh transport after `getUpdates` conflicts, with regression coverage and a shipped changelog entry. The PR's exact approach was not merged, but the reported bug is already addressed on `main` and released.
