## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement this PR’s TUI client-affinity/session-reattach design, and recently shipped gateway behavior explicitly keeps UI/TUI main-session sends on the internal webchat route instead of attaching a TUI-specific origin. The PR also has an unresolved correctness concern in review about reattaching short replies onto the wrong explicit session.
