## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` appears to have fixed the macOS LaunchAgent lifecycle and log-directory parts of the report, and it now documents/provider-defaults the `models.providers.*.api` surface better, but there is not strong evidence that `gateway.auth.token` and `gateway.remote.token` are automatically synchronized after config patches/restarts. The config schema still allows a missing `models.providers.*.api`, so the validation request in issue 4 is not fully implemented either.

## Evidence

- **Start/stop lifecycle appears fixed on main:** `runDaemonStart()` now attempts LaunchAgent recovery when the service is installed but not loaded, instead of leaving `gateway start` dead-ended after a prior unload.
  - file: [src/cli/daemon-cli/lifecycle.ts:152](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/7d9172d5/src/cli/daemon-cli/lifecycle.ts#L152)

- **LaunchAgent recovery/repair exists on main:** `repairLaunchAgentBootstrap()` explicitly re-enables, bootstraps, and kickstarts the LaunchAgent; `stopLaunchAgent()` now disables/restarts cleanly instead of always booting out the plist.
  - file: [src/daemon/launchd.ts:318](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/7d9172d5/src/daemon/launchd.ts#L318)
