## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` already contains the bundled cron/forum-topic and `NO_REPLY` cleanup, but it still does not honor `HTTP_PROXY`/`HTTPS_PROXY` for strict `web_fetch`; the PR's remaining web-fetch change is unmerged, and the existing review comment points out a plausible redirect bug in the proposed approach.
