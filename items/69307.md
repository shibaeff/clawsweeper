## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not expose a `before_tts`-style modifying hook, and the existing hooks do not provide the narrow seam requested: final replies go through TTS before normal outbound delivery hooks, `message_sending` only rewrites outbound text/transcript after that point, and `reply_dispatch` is a terminal full-dispatch takeover rather than a lightweight pre-TTS transform. This is consistent with project direction to expand plugin APIs when a useful capability cannot yet be built as a plugin.

## Evidence
