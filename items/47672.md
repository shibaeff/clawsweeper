## Summary

Current `main` still lacks the PR's pre-doctor progress wiring. `openclaw doctor` continues to print a static update note and call `runGatewayUpdate(...)` without a `progress` controller, even though the update runner and the regular `openclaw update` path already support step progress. This remains a live, narrowly scoped UX fix rather than a cleanup close candidate.
