## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still uses an empty synthetic text block when `dropThinkingBlocks()` strips a historical assistant turn down to nothing, and OpenClaw's Anthropic transport already drops empty text blocks before sending provider payloads. That makes the PR's root-cause claim plausible and the proposed fix actionable rather than stale or already implemented.
