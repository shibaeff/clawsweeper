## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` already supports `memorySearch.outputDimensionality` for Gemini Embedding 2 and partial multimodal indexing, but that support is limited to image/audio files under `memorySearch.extraPaths`. Session/conversation history indexing is still text-only, and the implementation does not cover video/PDF or chat attachments as requested.
