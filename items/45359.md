## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has fresh Codex/GPT-5.4 auth/fallback hardening, but I did not find strong proof that it specifically fixes the reported sequence: `openai-codex/gpt-5.4` returning intermittent `server_error` after OAuth refresh until the user switches models away and back. The nearest evidence is generic refresh/state hardening plus generic `server_error` fallback handling, not a direct reproduction or regression test for this bug.
