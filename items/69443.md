## Summary

Current main clearly separates `sessionKey` from `to`, and it has regression coverage around preserving spawned-subagent delivery context, but the issue report itself is truncated and does not provide the actual failing RPC payload. I could not verify with high confidence whether this is an already-fixed core bug, a still-open higher-level caller bug, or misuse of the raw `agent` RPC.

## Evidence

  - file: [src/gateway/protocol/schema/agent.ts:131](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/9577de2da7dfb6234cf30e3b879940067804e26e/src/gateway/protocol/schema/agent.ts#L131)

  - file: [docs/concepts/agent-loop.md:24](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/9577de2da7dfb6234cf30e3b879940067804e26e/docs/concepts/agent-loop.md#L24)
