## Summary

Current `main` already suppresses commentary/tool-use assistant text from user-visible delivery, and the Feishu path only delivers the final visible answer rather than exposing the intermediate commentary turn as a second reply.

## Evidence

- **Commentary replies suppressed during streaming updates:** `handleMessageUpdate` resolves the assistant phase and returns early for `commentary`, so pre-tool visible assistant text is not emitted as partial or block user output.
  - file: [src/agents/pi-embedded-subscribe.handlers.messages.ts:378](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/c070509b7f8fdbe479e5d829643782c272e30e83/src/agents/pi-embedded-subscribe.handlers.messages.ts#L378)

  - sha: [c070509b7f8f](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/commit/c070509b7f8fdbe479e5d829643782c272e30e83)
- **Commentary replies suppressed at message end/finalization:** `handleMessageEnd` also exits early for `commentary`, preventing those turns from being finalized into `assistantTexts` or user-visible final replies.
  - file: [src/agents/pi-embedded-subscribe.handlers.messages.ts:618](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/c070509b7f8fdbe479e5d829643782c272e30e83/src/agents/pi-embedded-subscribe.handlers.messages.ts#L618)
