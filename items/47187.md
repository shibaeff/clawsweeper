## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` already has the `.context-notice*` styling from the PR, but the rest of #47187 is not implemented on `main`: there is no `resetChatTransientUi()` helper or equivalent send-path reset, and the `details/summary` marker cleanup in the PR is also absent from current CSS.

## Evidence
