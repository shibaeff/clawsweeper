## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` shows some related improvements, but not enough evidence to conservatively close the report: the WhatsApp reconnect path still resets retry state after any connection lasting more than the 60s heartbeat window, the session-key complaint is only partially addressed by newer session-tool UX, and I did not find strong on-main proof that the message-tool false-abort or xai-auth spam reports are fixed.

## Evidence

- **WhatsApp retry state resets before watchdog-driven reconnects can accumulate:** `resolveCloseDecision()` zeroes `reconnectAttempts` whenever the current connection has been alive longer than `heartbeatSeconds`. With the monitor defaults of `heartbeatSeconds = 60` and `messageTimeoutMs = 30 * 60 * 1000`, stale long-lived connections re-enter retry as attempt 1 instead of accumulating toward `maxAttempts`. That does not strongly support closing the reconnect-storm report.
