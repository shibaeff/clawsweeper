## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` explicitly fails closed for loopback-source `trusted-proxy` requests, documents same-host loopback proxies as unsupported for that mode, and the PR's unconditional localhost bypass conflicts with that security posture. The timeline's cross-reference to #69066 also suggests the ACP/internal-caller need is being treated as a separate design problem rather than a safe one-line merge.
