## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still uses the coarse `!result.delivered` hook fallback, still lacks the PR's `announceToMain` bridge and targeted announcement-policy tests, and still retains the default-name `Hook Hook` formatting path. The PR discussion shows the author already rebased and addressed earlier review nits; the remaining question is maintainer judgment on the `deliveryAttempted` policy, not whether the item is obsolete.
