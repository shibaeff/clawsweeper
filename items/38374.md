## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement Viber support, the request is concrete and actionable, and the repo’s current docs/vision show both that messaging channels are a supported plugin surface and that improving major messaging-channel support remains in scope. That is not strong evidence for `implemented_on_main`, `cannot_reproduce`, `clawhub`, `incoherent`, or `stale_insufficient_info`.
