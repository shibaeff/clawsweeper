## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not show a strong fix for this report: cron announce output only prefers `finalAssistantVisibleText` when the channel plugin opts in, WhatsApp does not opt in, and the direct cron delivery tests still assert multi-payload text delivery for WhatsApp even when a final assistant-visible text exists. That means the reported summary/delivery flattening path is still plausible rather than clearly resolved on `main`.
