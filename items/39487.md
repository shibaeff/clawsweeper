## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still lacks the Feishu liveness/status wiring proposed here, and the PR discussion identifies unresolved correctness gaps in the submitted patch, so there is not strong evidence for any conservative auto-close reason.

## Evidence

The PR's proposed `statusSink` plumbing is not present on current `main`.

  - file: [extensions/feishu/src/monitor.account.ts:235](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/017252e4f8e76e6e95904bf8f798097de8dd36f8/extensions/feishu/src/monitor.account.ts#L235)
