## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still starts `sessions_spawn` child runs without `inputProvenance`, while adjacent inter-session paths already attach provenance metadata. I found no `sourceTool: "sessions_spawn"` plumbing or tests on `main` that would satisfy the issue’s acceptance criteria.
