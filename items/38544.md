## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement this PR's proposed warning suppression. Instead, it now enforces a newer duplicate-precedence policy: bundled stays ahead of auto-discovered globals, explicit installed globals can beat bundled, and distinct duplicate ids still emit a warning. That means the PR is not implemented-on-main and is not safe to close as obsolete.
