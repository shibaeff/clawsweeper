## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` at `151befb90b903f52dce9f4951991d39b9df8b358` still lacks the PR's reserved-session-namespace guard, so external hooks can still supply session keys that land in internal `subagent:`, `acp:`, or `cron:` namespaces after agent-scope parsing/rebinding. The PR discussion also shows the author addressed the review-discovered gaps on the branch, so this is still actionable work rather than an obsolete or incoherent PR.
