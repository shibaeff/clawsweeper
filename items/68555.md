## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still implements and logs the fallback behavior this PR is trying to change, and that behavior is also documented in the shipped changelog as intentional. That means this is neither already implemented nor obsolete, but it is also not strong enough to auto-close under the allowed cleanup reasons.
