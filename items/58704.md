## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still has the mechanics described in the report: same-phone WhatsApp DMs are admitted as inbound, echo filtering only drops tracked outbound message IDs, followup queues are keyed by stable `sessionKey`, and session reset rewrites queued followups onto the new session instead of clearing them.
