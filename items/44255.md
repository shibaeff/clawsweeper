## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement this PR’s outbound-message secret-redaction work: outbound send/poll paths still forward raw content, CLI startup does not initialize dynamic secret scanning, and `src/logging/redact.ts` remains the existing regex-only redactor.
