## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still runs Active Memory as an awaited blocking `before_prompt_build` sub-agent on the normal embedded-run path, and Telegram’s current changes are watchdog/restart and offset-persistence mitigations rather than a fix that isolates recall work from polling. I found no strong evidence on `main` or in shipped notes that #65517 is fully implemented, obsolete, or safe to auto-close.

## Evidence
