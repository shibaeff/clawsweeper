## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still lacks the WhatsApp poll/reaction/event extraction and event-stream routing described by the PR, and the gated-group early return still skips the later `message:received` hook bridge. I found no evidence on `main` that this PR’s behavior was implemented elsewhere.
