## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` has partial Control UI/WebChat attachment support, but the inbound upload path is still image-only end to end, while broader document/file upload remains unimplemented. That matches the maintainer note added on April 25, 2026 that this issue should stay separate from the image-only work tracked in #51045.
