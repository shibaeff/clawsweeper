## Summary

Current `main` already implements the requested NVIDIA NIM reasoning-effort compatibility in the shared OpenAI-completions transport path: unsupported `minimal` is normalized to `low`, and `none` is omitted. That makes this unmerged provider-specific wrapper PR redundant.

## Evidence
