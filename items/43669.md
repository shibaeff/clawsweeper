## Summary

Current `main` has an adjacent teardown fix for the auto-retry/tool-result race, but it does not implement this PR’s post-`prompt()` idle wait. The claimed change is therefore not already on `main`, and I do not have high-confidence evidence that the underlying premature-snapshot/final-reply race is obsolete.
