## Summary

Current `main` already contains the reply-suppression and delivery-mirror filtering needed for this report: same-target `message.send` turns are suppressed in the final reply path, transcript-only `delivery-mirror` assistant messages are ignored, and regression tests cover the duplicate-message scenarios described here.
