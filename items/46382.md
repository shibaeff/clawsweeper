## Summary

Current `main` already handles streamed OpenAI-compatible tool-call arguments through OpenClaw’s boundary-aware transport, which finalizes with tolerant `parseStreamingJson(...)` instead of strict `JSON.parse(...)`. The proposed local `@mariozechner/pi-ai@0.52.9` patch is obsolete, and the PR also remains burdened by unrelated/security-sensitive files called out in review.
