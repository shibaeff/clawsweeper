## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still builds `session-memory` filenames and fallback/header timestamps from `Date.toISOString()`, so the reported UTC-vs-local-date bug remains in shipped code. The PR stays relevant: it targets a real user-visible behavior gap, its earlier review concerns were addressed in-thread, and the remaining red CI discussed on the PR was a stale macOS timeout rather than evidence that the change is wrong.

## Evidence

That matches the bug report, not the proposed fix.
