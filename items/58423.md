## Summary

Current `main` already handles webchat image attachments through `chat.send`: the gateway forwards them via `replyOptions.images`/`imageOrder`, the reply pipeline treats those as inbound media even without `ctx.MediaPath`, and the transcript layer persists `MediaPath`/`MediaPaths` for the saved files. This behavior is also present in release `v2026.4.23`.
