## Summary

Current `main` does not support the reported cross-agent identity collision theory. Discord routing is resolved per `channel + accountId`, the resulting session key is still agent-scoped, and session persistence is stored/read from per-agent stores derived from that agent-scoped key. On current `main`, separate Discord accounts bound to separate agents in the same channel do not share a session store or identity lookup path.

## Evidence
