## Summary

Current main already decouples pending approval replay from native approval handler startup. Replay delivery/list failures are logged instead of rejecting startup, which matches the reported gateway-restart failure mode.

## Evidence

- **Replay is asynchronous and errors are swallowed into logs:** `startPendingApprovalReplay` wraps pending-approval replay in a `.catch(...)` that logs `error replaying pending approvals` instead of propagating the error.
