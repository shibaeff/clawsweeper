## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still applies strict SSRF DNS pinning to `web_fetch`, and I did not find a landed GitHub-specific or false-positive-resolution fix in the relevant code, docs, tests, changelog, or `v2026.4.23..main` diff. The report is recent and plausible, but it looks environment-sensitive rather than conclusively obsolete.
