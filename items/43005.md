## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still hard-codes the cross-origin redirect header allowlist, still strips `x-api-key` in the fetch-guard tests, and exposes no config/schema/docs surface for an `additionalSafeHeaders` or `security.fetchGuard` override. This enhancement is not implemented on `main` as of `41c5ffc5d5f4dc0f1df6f2c9a7efd8ce6a627f00`.

## Evidence

- **Hard-coded safe header allowlist:** `CROSS_ORIGIN_REDIRECT_SAFE_HEADERS` is a fixed `Set` and does not read config or runtime options.
  - file: [src/infra/net/redirect-headers.ts:3](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/41c5ffc5d5f4dc0f1df6f2c9a7efd8ce6a627f00/src/infra/net/redirect-headers.ts#L3)
