## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` at `c070509b7f8fdbe479e5d829643782c272e30e83` does not implement cron/heartbeat `preHook` support, and the PR head still has an unresolved contract bug: `cron.update` advertises `preHook: false` as the clear sentinel, but normalization drops `false` instead of clearing the stored hook.
