## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` shows recent CI and test-harness churn that may mitigate parts of the report, but the issue describes a shared regression across loader, Feishu, Google OAuth, and Windows. I do not have strong evidence that all of those surfaces are fixed on current `main`, and I could not run the reported `pnpm` repro in this checkout because `pnpm` is unavailable.
