## Summary

Current `main` already suppresses the duplicate reply path after successful `message.send(...)`: transcript-only `delivery-mirror` assistant messages are ignored, successful messaging-tool sends are tracked, and `message_end` block replies are skipped when they would duplicate that delivered text. The same behavior shipped in `v2026.4.22` and remains unchanged on current `main`.
