## Summary

## Close Comment

Closing from a Codex review: this is already implemented on current `main`, and the relevant Telegram polling fixes are present in shipped release `v2026.4.22` (`00bd2cf7a376f1fba26291c6c4766f1f15cbdfa5`). Restart paths now serialize `stopChannel` before `startChannel` for both config reloads and health-monitor restarts (`src/gateway/server-reload-handlers.ts:175`, `src/gateway/channel-health-monitor.ts:160`, `src/gateway/server-channels.ts:555`). On the Telegram side, shutdown/restart aborts the in-flight polling fetch and waits for runner/bot teardown before the cycle ends (`extensions/telegram/src/bot-core.ts:181`, `extensions/telegram/src/polling-session.ts:291`), and `getUpdates` 409 conflicts now force a fresh transport instead of retrying on the stale connection (`extensions/telegram/src/polling-session.ts:366`, covered by `extensions/telegram/src/polling-session.test.ts:917`). The changelog entries for restart teardown, poll restart cleanup, and 409 conflict recovery are also already in the release (`CHANGELOG.md:207`, `CHANGELOG.md:2395`, `CHANGELOG.md:2881`, `CHANGELOG.md:3667`).
