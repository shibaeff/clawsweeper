## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still has the pre-PR behavior: fresh local `not-paired` devices are eligible for silent pairing, silent approvals still pass the requested scopes as `callerScopes`, and the new fresh-device approval regression coverage from PR #58367 is not present.
