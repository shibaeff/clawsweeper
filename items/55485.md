## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` only implements part of this PR's scope: `tools.web.fetch.ssrfPolicy.allowRfc2544BenchmarkRange` exists, but the proposed `tools.web.search.citationRedirect.ssrfPolicy` and `messages.remoteMedia.ssrfPolicy` surfaces are not present on `main`, and the PR discussion still has unresolved correctness concerns around cache identity and send-path coverage.
