## Summary

Current `main` in the checked-out repo does not implement this PR's change. Generated videos are still saved with `agents.defaults.mediaMaxMb` when configured, otherwise `saveMediaBuffer` falls back to the generic 5 MB store default; the proposed video-specific 16 MB cap is not present.
