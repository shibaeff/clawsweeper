## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not show an implemented fix for deleted-account relink recovery: OpenClaw still reuses the saved ClawHub token, forwards backend errors from ClawHub, and only special-cases 404s. The reported "client block" remains plausible and not disproven by the current code.
