## Summary

Current `main` already preserves WebChat/Gateway image attachments for text-only or non-vision session models by offloading them as `media://inbound/*` refs at the Gateway layer, with regression coverage for both plain text-only sessions and agent-scoped non-default sessions. That supersedes this PR's runner-level `existingImages` passthrough change.

## Evidence
