## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` already implements the message/session lifecycle-hook part of the request, but the orphan-session continuity part is still not solved in the session UI/index path. `sessions.list` still builds its result only from `sessions.json`, while `doctor` explicitly treats unreferenced `.jsonl` transcripts as orphan files that are outside active session history and only offers to archive them.

## Evidence

- **Session list still comes only from the store index:** `listSessionsFromStore` starts from `Object.entries(store)` and maps those rows into the returned session list; there is no transcript-directory scan or orphan-session merge in this path.
  - file: [src/gateway/session-utils.ts:1461](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/9b7f6250f43bbc1816cdaf26e8c07116dfa4975f/src/gateway/session-utils.ts#L1461)
