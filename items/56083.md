## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still does not apply `messages.responsePrefix` in the cron direct-delivery path, while normal reply delivery and heartbeat delivery do apply it. The PR is addressing a real remaining gap, and the discussion shows the contributor already handled the idempotency edge cases raised in review.

## Evidence

- **Cron direct delivery still sends raw payloads:** In current `main`, `dispatchCronDelivery()` builds `rawPayloads`, strips silent-token markers, and passes `payloadsForDelivery` straight to `deliverOutboundPayloads()` without resolving `messages.responsePrefix` or prepending it anywhere in the cron path.
