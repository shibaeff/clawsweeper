## Summary

Current `main` already supports Gemma 4 model IDs in the native Google provider, including forward-compat model resolution and Gemma 4 thinking payload normalization. The changelog shows this shipped in `v2026.4.7`, and the issue discussion points to the landing PR/commit that superseded the follow-up work.

## Evidence

## Risks / Open Questions

- A follow-up comment notes one narrower remaining edge case: fully-qualified IDs like `models/gemma-4-31b-it` may bypass the Gemma 4 normalization branch. That is separate from this issue's requested bare model-ID support.
