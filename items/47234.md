## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement per-binding `groupScope`, the companion IRC send-path change is also absent, and the PR discussion surfaced unresolved correctness gaps around outbound routing and Telegram topic overrides rather than evidence that the change is obsolete or already shipped.
