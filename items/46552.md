## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not contain the proposed `docs/concepts/queue.md` additions, and the runtime/docs evidence supports the PR’s clarifications about `partial` vs `block` streaming and non-streaming `steer` falling back to a queued followup.

## Evidence

There is no `Steer and partial streaming` section and no troubleshooting note about per-session `/queue` overrides or non-streaming fallback.
  - file: [docs/concepts/queue.md:76](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/0bef73d151bd0a8d547b28bf16c3aa2e21078e0f/docs/concepts/queue.md#L76)

That matches the PR’s follow-up revision after the Greptile review and supports the value of adding the same distinction to queue docs.
  - file: [docs/channels/discord.md:560](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/0bef73d151bd0a8d547b28bf16c3aa2e21078e0f/docs/channels/discord.md#L560)
