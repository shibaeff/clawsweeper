## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement the PR's `lastGood`-preferred initial auth-profile selection. `resolveSessionAuthProfileOverride()` still chooses the first non-cooldown entry from provider order for fresh auto selection, and the current tests do not cover the proposed `lastGood` scenarios.
