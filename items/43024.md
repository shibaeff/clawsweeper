## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement a first-class or implicit PPIO provider. OpenClaw can talk to PPIO manually through the existing OpenAI-compatible custom-provider path, but the PR’s requested behavior (`PPIO_API_KEY` auto-enables PPIO with built-in discovery/fallback) is not present on `main`.
