## Summary

Current main does document and enforce that `sessionTarget: "main"` cron jobs are system-event heartbeats, while agent-turn prompts and channel announce delivery belong to isolated/custom sessions. But the exact control token `ANNOUNCE_SKIP` is still treated as a suppressed reply globally, and I did not find any current warning or validation that prevents a main-session cron payload from steering the model into that silent reply. That is not strong enough to auto-close as fixed or obsolete.

## Evidence
