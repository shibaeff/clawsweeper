## Summary

Current `main` already bounds the reported Telegram silent-polling stall path: stuck `getUpdates` calls are hard-timed out after 45s, polling liveness is tracked and force-restarted, and the changelog shows the primary fix shipped in `v2026.3.13` with additional Telegram polling hardening in `v2026.4.18`.

## Close Comment

Closing as implemented based on a Codex review of current `main`. The reported Telegram silent-polling delay path is already addressed: stuck `getUpdates` requests are hard-timed out after 45s in `extensions/telegram/src/request-timeouts.ts:1` and `extensions/telegram/src/bot-core.ts:181`, and polling still has liveness-based forced restart logic in `extensions/telegram/src/polling-session.ts:313`. This was shipped in `v2026.3.13` per `CHANGELOG.md:2317`, with additional Telegram polling transport/liveness hardening later in `v2026.4.18` (`CHANGELOG.md:487`). The separate empty-WhatsApp restart-loop concern appears to be a different issue. If you still hit long Telegram delivery delays on a current release, please open a new issue with the exact version and fresh logs around `getUpdates` timeouts / `Polling stall detected` so maintainers can inspect the remaining path.
