## Summary

Current `main` does not implement `hooks.mappings[].sessionTarget`. Hook mapping config/schema/runtime still omit the field, gateway hook dispatch still hardcodes isolated execution, and the surrounding cron/session rules still treat `sessionTarget: "main"` as a special path. This PR is not obsolete and should stay open for maintainer review rather than being conservatively closed.
