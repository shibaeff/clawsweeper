## Summary

Current `main` has explicit handling for pairing-request supersession: it re-resolves the live pending `requestId` before surfacing pairing-required errors, keeps identical retries on the same `requestId`, and tests the replacement-ID race directly. That is enough evidence that the stale/unknown-`requestId` pairing failure reported against `2026.4.19-beta.1` is implemented on `main`, though I could not tie it to a released tag.

## Evidence
