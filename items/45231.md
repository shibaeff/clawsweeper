## Summary

Current `main` does not implement the proposed `before_agent_run` hook or its reservation-based reply-path changes. The closest existing behavior is `before_agent_reply`, which has different semantics: it receives only `cleanedBody` and short-circuits by returning a synthetic reply rather than a narrow `{ skip }` veto immediately before the default run.
