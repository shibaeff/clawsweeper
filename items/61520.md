## Summary

Keep open. On current `main` (`7b5307acfc347a4e6d6647156600ee4b8d6eaa37`), the config IO path still silently swallows multiple read/write/audit failures, including the specific health-state and audit-log cases described in the issue. The linked PR attempts in the issue timeline do not provide evidence of a landed fix on `main`.

## Risks / Open Questions
