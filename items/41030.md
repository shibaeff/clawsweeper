## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not contain this PR's fresh-event suppression, and the gateway health design has evolved toward `lastTransportActivityAt` plus run-activity heartbeats. That makes the proposed `lastEventAt` fix look partially stale, but it does not give high-confidence proof that the underlying false-restart report is fully resolved or that the PR should be auto-closed.
