## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not contain the PR's two substantive fixes: `message-tool` still has no `requesterAgentIdOverride` path when `agentSessionKey` is absent, and `sessions_send` A2A announce still sends without forwarding requester `sessionKey`/parsed `agentId`.
