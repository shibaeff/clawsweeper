## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not contain the Android assistant hardening from this PR, and the submitted patch still has an unresolved correctness concern from review about suppressing fresh assistant launches after restore. That is not strong evidence for a conservative close.

## Evidence

- **Current main still replays assistant intents without any restore/fingerprint guard:** `MainActivity` handles the launch intent in `onCreate` and again in `onNewIntent`, but there is no saved-instance-state fingerprinting or restore-specific suppression on current `main`. That means the PR's proposed behavior is not already implemented.
