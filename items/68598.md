## Summary

Current `main` treats `entities/`, `concepts/`, and `syntheses/` as maintained wiki page buckets, not an in-core auto-extraction pipeline; a source-to-wiki extractor is better scoped as a ClawHub skill/plugin than an OpenClaw core issue.

## Evidence

- **Docs define the folders as page groups, not an extractor pipeline:** The memory-wiki docs describe the vault layout and page groups: `sources/` for imported raw material, `entities/` / `concepts/` / `syntheses/` for maintained wiki pages, and `reports/` for generated dashboards. They do not document a built-in source-to-entity/concept/synthesis extractor.
