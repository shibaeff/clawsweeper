## Summary

Current `main` still models WhatsApp and voice-call phone-number fields as plain strings/E.164 arrays, and the generated channel SecretRef policy only marks WhatsApp `creds.json` as unsupported rather than adding SecretRef-capable phone-number surfaces. The maintainer comment on the issue matches the current code, so there is not strong evidence to close this item.
