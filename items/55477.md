## Summary

Current `main` does not stamp `session_key`, `message_channel`, or `context_limit` into LiteLLM-bound request metadata, and the existing provider-plugin transport hooks do not expose the needed session/channel/context fields to implement this externally. This PR should stay open for maintainer review rather than being auto-closed.

## Evidence

- **No session-metadata wrapper in current embedded runner:** The `runEmbeddedAttempt` stream wrapper chain on `main` applies cache, transcript, tool-call, yield, malformed-tool-call, payload-logger, stop-reason, and idle-timeout wrappers, but there is no wrapper that injects request metadata such as `session_key`, `message_channel`, or `context_limit`. The proposed PR hunk is not present in current `main`.
