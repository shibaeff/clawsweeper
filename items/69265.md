## Summary

Current `main` now documents and enforces the hot-reload boundaries this issue asked to clarify: `agent`/`agents`/`models`/`routing`, channels, skills, bindings, and most other config hot-apply, while `gateway.*`, `plugins`, `discovery`, and `canvasHost` require a restart. The clarification landed on `main` after the issue was filed, so this is resolved there even though it does not appear to be in the latest tagged release yet.
