## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not show `Created` or `Last Used` in `openclaw devices list`, while the underlying pairing data already carries the needed timestamps. The PR is still additive, relevant, and its only noted review concerns were minor P2 items that the PR author appears to have addressed in the follow-up commit from the provided timeline.
