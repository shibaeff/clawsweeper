## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` adds Gmail webhook setup/auto-renew around `gog`, but it still relies on external `gog` OAuth state and only validates Gmail watch startup. The issue’s requested recovery baseline for revoked/expired Google auth on headless instances, plus clear Gmail/Drive recovery validation, is not fully implemented or documented yet.

## Evidence
