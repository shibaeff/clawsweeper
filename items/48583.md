## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not contain the PR's embedded-run parent-finalizer, and the PR as proposed conflicts with current subagent cleanup contracts: run-mode spawns default to `cleanup: "keep"`, delete-mode cleanup is intentionally retried on gateway failure, and current tests encode narrower run-mode orchestrator behavior than the PR's blanket delete-on-parent-exit logic.

## Evidence

- **Run-mode spawns default to keep:** `sessions_spawn` defaults `cleanup` to `keep`, and `spawnSubagent` preserves `keep` for `spawnMode === "run"` unless delete is explicitly requested. That makes the PR's `cleanup === "delete" || spawnMode === "run"` finalizer a behavior change, not just leak cleanup.
