## Summary

Current OpenClaw already covers the reported image-describe failure modes: explicit `image describe --model <provider/model>` routing goes through media understanding, shared `tools.media.models` config is read, MiniMax text models fall back to `MiniMax-VL-01` for image understanding, and reasoning-only empty image responses are retried to recover visible text. The relevant fixes are documented in the `2026.4.23` changelog and present on current `main`.
