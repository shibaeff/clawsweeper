## Summary

Current `main` already implements Telegram polling auto-recovery for dropped/stalled long-poll sessions. The Telegram monitor now restarts recoverable polling failures with backoff, force-restarts stalled `getUpdates` cycles, rebuilds the transport after recoverable errors and 409 conflicts, and applies a hard timeout to `getUpdates`. The changelog shows the first relevant fix shipped in `v2026.3.22`, with additional polling hardening in `v2026.4.20`.
