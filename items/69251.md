## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still routes non-Windows `exec host=node` through `/bin/sh -lc`, and the node-host approval planner still fail-closes that wrapper form for file-backed payloads with `SYSTEM_RUN_DENIED: approval cannot safely bind this interpreter/runtime command`. The only nearby shipped fix in this area, #69308, addressed non-interactive `$SHELL` fallback for local exec and did not change the node-host wrapper or approval-binding path.
