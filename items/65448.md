## Summary

Current `main` already includes the Gemini 3 Google reasoning fix and the same behavior shipped in `v2026.4.23`: OpenClaw strips invalid negative Google thinking budgets, maps Gemini 3/3.1 reasoning to `thinkingLevel`, documents the behavior, and covers it with regression tests. The bundled Google provider also owns the `google-vertex` alias path, so the fix applies to the reported surface.
