## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still does not expose `apply_patch` for non-OpenAI providers, so the reported Kimi repro is not implemented on `main` and cannot be closed as fixed or obsolete. The issue is also recent enough that `stale_insufficient_info` does not apply.
