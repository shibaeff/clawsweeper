## Summary

Not safe to close. Current `main` has adjacent empty-turn handling, but the shipped fix and current regression coverage only cover `stopReason="error"` zero-output turns. This issue reports `stopReason="stop"`, zero payloads, and no provider dispatch, and current tests explicitly mark that `stopReason="stop"` case as out of scope.
