## Summary

Current main already avoids permanent cron failure for model-exhaustion/transient provider errors: recurring jobs stay enabled and are rescheduled with backoff, and one-shot jobs retry transient failures before disabling. The exact reset-time parsing proposed in the issue is not implemented, but the reported permanent-failure behavior is already addressed.
