## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still appears to expose sandbox write tools for `workspaceAccess: "none"` even though the sandbox workspace is mounted/read as read-only and the filesystem bridge rejects writes unless access is `"rw"`. That matches the reported tool-surface mismatch rather than showing an implemented fix.
