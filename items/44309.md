## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still implements `sessions_send` as conversation-style agent-to-agent messaging with reply-back and announce behavior, and it exposes no dispatch-only flag or separate one-way delivery primitive. This is core session-tool behavior, not something a ClawHub plugin can realistically provide.

## Evidence
