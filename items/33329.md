## Summary

Keep open. Current main already has Copilot, Hugging Face, and Ollama discovery toggles in config/runtime and tests, and Bedrock discovery is documented under the migrated plugin-owned path, but the user-facing docs/config reference still do not document the Copilot/Hugging Face/Ollama discovery toggles requested by the issue.
