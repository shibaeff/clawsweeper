## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not show a landed fix for the reported Discord ACP `thread_binding_invalid` / `ACP_TURN_FAILED` behavior. The prompt-contract part of the report is partly explained by current ACP expecting `prompt` content arrays rather than raw strings, but the underlying Discord thread-binding and ACP turn-failure concerns are still plausible on `main`, and no post-issue code change in the relevant files proves they were fixed.
