## Summary

Current `main` now treats isolated cron model/run failures as real cron errors instead of reporting `ok`: fatal error payloads and run-level `meta.error` are converted into `status: "error"`, and cron state persistence updates `lastStatus`, `consecutiveErrors`, and failure alerts accordingly.
