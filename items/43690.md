## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still sends Feishu card replies with the original markdown tables, and the card failure paths only log/reset instead of falling back to plain text. The reported behavior remains plausible on `28fc03c386e14db502429bbe7e062cbe90439ca1`.

## Evidence
