## Summary

Current `main` already has inbound message deduplication at dispatch entry, and the pre-reply failover/live-switch paths now resolve to final payloads instead of repeating the same inbound run as an unhandled lane error.

## Evidence

- **Inbound dedupe cache exists on current main:** `src/auto-reply/reply/inbound-dedupe.ts` defines a shared inbound dedupe cache with a 20-minute TTL and keys it by channel/account/peer/session/message id.
  - file: [src/auto-reply/reply/inbound-dedupe.ts:11](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/11ad1919edc7278f9d3e36c87c56bd3cb52c6e41/src/auto-reply/reply/inbound-dedupe.ts#L11)
  - command: `nl -ba src/auto-reply/reply/inbound-dedupe.ts | sed -n '1,140p'`
- **Duplicate or inflight inbound deliveries are skipped before dispatch:** `dispatchReplyFromConfig` claims inbound dedupe before doing reply work, immediately skips `duplicate`/`inflight` deliveries, and commits the dedupe claim after a completed dispatch.
  - file: [src/auto-reply/reply/dispatch-from-config.ts:270](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/11ad1919edc7278f9d3e36c87c56bd3cb52c6e41/src/auto-reply/reply/dispatch-from-config.ts#L270)
  - command: `nl -ba src/auto-reply/reply/dispatch-from-config.ts | sed -n '266,276p'; nl -ba src/auto-reply/reply/dispatch-from-config.ts | sed -n '1148,1165p'`
- **Pre-reply provider failures no longer have to bubble out as lane errors:** `runAgentTurnWithFallback` converts pre-reply failure/fallback exhaustion into a final user-visible payload instead of leaving the turn as an unhandled error path.
  - file: [src/auto-reply/reply/agent-runner-execution.ts:1579](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/11ad1919edc7278f9d3e36c87c56bd3cb52c6e41/src/auto-reply/reply/agent-runner-execution.ts#L1579)
