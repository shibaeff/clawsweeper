## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` covers parts of the discussion with persistent session-state handling and first-class local backup/verify flows, but it does not fully implement the broader 'golden path' ask around durable off-site backups and explicit restore-drill workflows. The issue is also broader than a pure ClawHub/plugin request, so there is not strong enough evidence to close conservatively.
