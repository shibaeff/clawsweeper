## Summary

Current `main` already ships Microsoft Teams passwordless auth for certificate-based and federated/managed-identity deployments, with schema support, runtime implementation, docs, and tests. This draft PR appears superseded by the shipped `authType: "federated"` implementation, even though the final config shape differs from the draft’s proposed field names.
