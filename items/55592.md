## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still implements message deletion as a Control UI-only local hide backed by `localStorage`, and the gateway/protocol on `main` still has no `sessions.truncate` mutation. That means the PR's stated bugfix is not implemented on `main`, and the reported transcript/UI divergence remains plausible. The PR discussion also shows substantial review-driven hardening rather than an obsolete or incoherent change.
