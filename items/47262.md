## Close Comment

Closing based on a Codex review of current `main`. The Telegram send/finalization path now avoids duplicate resend on ambiguous or post-connect `sendMessage` failures (`extensions/telegram/src/send.ts:599`, `extensions/telegram/src/lane-delivery-text-deliverer.ts:257`), with regression coverage for the timeout/no-duplicate cases (`extensions/telegram/src/bot-message-dispatch.test.ts:3445`, `extensions/telegram/src/send.test.ts:1124`). The polling side was also hardened after the reported `2026.3.13` environment: the default stall threshold is now 120s and polling transport recovery rebuilds dirty/conflicted `getUpdates` sessions instead of looping on a terminated old session (`extensions/telegram/src/polling-session.ts:25`, `extensions/telegram/src/polling-session.ts:370`, `CHANGELOG.md:400`, `CHANGELOG.md:496`). These fixes were shipped by `v2026.4.20`. If anyone still sees `embedded_run_agent_end: terminated` on `v2026.4.20` or newer, please open a fresh issue with the current version, exact provider/model, and fresh logs, since that would likely be a newer provider/runtime transport failure rather than this older Telegram resend/stall path.
