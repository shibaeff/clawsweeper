## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still sends unmanaged `openclaw gateway restart` through `process.kill(pid, "SIGUSR1")` on Windows, and this PR’s touched area (`src/infra/restart.ts`) does not cover that CLI restart path. The latest review comments also identify unresolved restart-coordination regressions in the proposed fallback path.
