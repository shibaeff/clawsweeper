## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` added a global `messages.suppressToolErrors` knob, but the implementation explicitly does not suppress mutating tool failures, and `message`/send-style tools are classified as mutating. That means the issue's cited case of failed `message send` errors still reaches chat surfaces, including Telegram partial streaming paths.

## Evidence
