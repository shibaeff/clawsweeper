## Summary

Current `main` already contains a fix for malformed streamed tool-call arguments on OpenAI-compatible transports, and Doubao/Volcengine is wired through that transport. The reported `Missing required parameter` failures on v2026.3.28 are implemented on `main`, but the fix is still unreleased as of v2026.4.23.

## Evidence
