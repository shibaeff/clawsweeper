## Summary

Current main already covers the reported loopback `openclaw devices list/approve` failure: the pre-auth handshake timeout is back at 10s, pairing failures now surface as explicit `pairing required` responses instead of a bare `1000 normal closure`, and the CLI has a local fallback for loopback pairing deadlocks.
