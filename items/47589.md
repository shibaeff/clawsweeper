## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still uses the broad bare-`remember` reminder guard, so memory-retention phrases like `"I'll remember your preference"` still match and can trigger the unscheduled-reminder note. The PR's narrower matcher and added test coverage are not present on `main` yet.
