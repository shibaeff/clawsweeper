## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not contain the proposed `msteams-channel-archive` plugin, the `channel_deleted` hook contract/runner, or Teams `channelDeleted` dispatch, so the PR's core behavior is not implemented on `main` and there is no strong basis to close it as obsolete.
