## Summary

Current `main` already suppresses commentary/intermediate assistant text from user-visible delivery between tool calls. The generic embedded subscriber drops `commentary`-phase partials and `message_end` replies before they reach channel-facing output, and regression tests cover both streaming partials and final message delivery.
