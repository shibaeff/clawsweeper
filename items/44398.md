## Summary

Current `main` already prevents health-monitor restart loops for unconfigured channel accounts. Unconfigured accounts are treated as unmanaged by the health policy, gateway channel setup marks unconfigured accounts with `configured: false`, resolver failures disable monitoring for that account, and there is a regression test that asserts unconfigured channels are skipped.
