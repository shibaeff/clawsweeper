## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still lacks the ACP timeout fallback proposed here: the ACP `sessions_spawn` path does not forward `runTimeoutSeconds`, `spawnAcpDirect` has no ACP timeout parameter/default resolver, and the config schema/types/docs do not expose `acp.defaultRunTimeoutSeconds`. The PR remains actionable rather than stale or already implemented.

## Evidence
