## Summary

Current `main` does not ship ModelScope support, and the available evidence is not strong enough to auto-close this PR. OpenClaw now has strong external provider-plugin and generic OpenAI-compatible-provider paths, but the project vision still treats major provider support as active product scope, so a `clawhub` close would be too aggressive without explicit maintainer direction.
