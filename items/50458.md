## Summary

Current `main` already fixes the bundled-plugin skill discovery regression by copying `SKILL.md` into `dist-runtime`, and the repo’s current QA/test coverage treats that as the required fix. The open PR’s broader `skills/**` copy behavior is not what `main` now intends; `main` explicitly still symlinks non-`SKILL.md` skill assets.
