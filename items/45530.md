## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement the PR's persistent TTS voice switching behavior. The TTS directive/result types on `main` have no `persist` flag, the shared directive parser does not recognize or return persistence metadata, the speech-core TTS prefs shape has no persisted per-provider voice map, and the current reply/Discord voice flows synthesize speech without any post-success voice persistence write.

## Evidence
