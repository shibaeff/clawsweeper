## Summary

Current `main` already contains shipped mitigations for the two reported hot paths: oversized session stores are pruned/capped during load to prevent gateway OOM and slow `sessions.list`, and `chat.history` is now aggressively bounded for Control UI safety. That matches the reported 600+ MB startup growth plus slow `sessions.list`/`chat.history` behavior on `2026.4.14` closely enough to close as fixed on `main`.
