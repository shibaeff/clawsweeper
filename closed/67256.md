## Summary

The reported empty `mediaUrl`/`mediaUrls` behavior is already covered by current OpenClaw: absolute workspace `MEDIA:` paths are normalized and preserved for outbound delivery, and the changelog says that fix shipped in `v2026.4.18`, which is newer than the issue's reported `v2026.4.14`.
