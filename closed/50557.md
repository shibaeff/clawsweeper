## Summary

Current `main` already has the requested session-store locking hardening: per-store serialization, atomic writes, stale-lock reclamation, watchdog/exit cleanup, doctor cleanup, and gateway single-instance enforcement. The report’s requested direction is implemented well enough on current OpenClaw.

## Evidence

- **Session-store writes are serialized per store and re-read under lock:** `updateSessionStore()` now always executes inside `withSessionStoreLock()`, re-reads the store under the lock, and the lock path is queued through `LOCK_QUEUES` so same-process concurrent writers serialize instead of racing on the `.lock` file.
