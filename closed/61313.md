## Summary

Current `main` no longer matches the reported routing blockers: explicit `host=node` from `tools.exec.host=auto` is implemented for unsandboxed sessions, agent-level `tools.exec` defaults are resolved and passed into the exec tool, and the `nodes invoke system.run` / `nodes run` paths are intentionally unsupported in favor of `exec host=node`. Release notes show the routing fixes shipped after `2026.4.2`, with the last related host-routing fix called out in `v2026.4.7`.
