## Summary

Current `main` already implements the reported Bailian/DashScope streaming-usage path: the transport records usage from terminal streaming chunks, DashScope/Qwen endpoints are explicitly treated as streaming-usage-compatible, and the shipped changelog records this fix in `v2026.3.22`.
