## Summary

Current `main` already preserves per-call `policy` overrides in `fetchWithWebToolsNetworkGuard`, scopes the trusted SSRF policy to the trusted wrapper only, and has tests that lock in the strict-default behavior. This PR's extra defaulting is unnecessary on current `main` and matches the security regression called out in review.

## Evidence
