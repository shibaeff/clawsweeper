## Summary

Current `main` already resolves Feishu group mention gating the way this issue asks for: open groups no longer require @mentions unless `requireMention` is explicitly set, and per-group `groups.<chat_id>` overrides with raw `oc_*` IDs are recognized.

## Evidence

- **Reply policy on current main:** `extensions/feishu/src/policy.ts:204` resolves `groups.<chat_id>.requireMention` first, then top-level/account config, and otherwise defaults `requireMention` to `false` when `groupPolicy` is `"open"`.
