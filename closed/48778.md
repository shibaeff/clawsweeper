## Summary

Current `main` already implements in-channel task interruption for active runs: inbound messages are checked for `/stop` and standalone abort phrases before normal reply handling, authorized aborts immediately cancel the active session/run, and the agent/tool runtime propagates cancellation through `AbortSignal` to active tool execution.
