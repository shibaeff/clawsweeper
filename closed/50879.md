## Summary

Current `main` no longer silently accepts `message.send` with poll fields. It now rejects that combination with an explicit error and routes Telegram polls through the dedicated `poll` action backed by `sendPollTelegram`.

## Evidence

- **Core runner blocks the broken path:** `runMessageAction` now throws when `action === "send"` and poll creation params are present, preventing the old `ok: true` text-only fallback.
