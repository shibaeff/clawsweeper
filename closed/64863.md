## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not contain the PR change, and the proposed one-file fix is not strong enough to close as already-implemented or obsolete: media transcription already classifies attachments from MIME/path metadata, while the separate Discord audio-preflight path still depends only on `content_type` and remains unaddressed by this PR.
