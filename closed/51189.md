## Summary

Issue #51189 appears fixed on current `main` and in the shipped `v2026.4.22` release: Feishu now routes single-agent replies through the shared `withReplyDispatcher` completion wrapper, the Feishu reply dispatcher explicitly closes streaming cards on idle/error, and regression tests cover the missing-final-reply path that would have left dispatch incomplete.
