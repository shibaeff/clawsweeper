## Summary

Close as implemented on current `main`. The current UI no longer relies on the proposed CSS-only fix: `ui/src/ui/chat/context-notice.ts` now renders the warning SVG with explicit `width="16"` and `height="16"`, and `ui/src/ui/chat/context-notice.test.ts` asserts those attributes. That is a stronger intrinsic-size fix than this PR's added `max-width`/`max-height` rules, and the notice is already wired through the live chat view on `main`.
