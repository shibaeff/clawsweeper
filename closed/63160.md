## Summary

Current `main` already routes injected WhatsApp send deps through the media-aware channel outbound runtime, and checked-in WhatsApp tests assert that `mediaUrl` reaches the dep path for `sendMedia` and `sendPayload`. This PR's bypass is obsolete on `main` and would skip a supported dep-override path.

## Evidence
