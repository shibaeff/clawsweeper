## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still sends Google video generation through `@google/genai`'s `generateVideos()` path with no visible `predictLongRunning` fallback, and the linked discussion says the proposed fix lives in PR #62343 rather than in shipped `main`. I did not find source evidence that issue #63008 is already implemented or obsolete.
