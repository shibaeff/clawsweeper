## Summary

Current `main` already suppresses the reported webchat/control-UI duplicate caused by `delivery-mirror`: redundant mirror transcript writes are skipped when they match the latest assistant message, and transcript-only OpenClaw assistant messages are ignored by visible streaming/block-reply paths.
