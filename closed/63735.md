## Summary

Current `main` now treats the QA scenario pack as optional for packaged/npm distributions, keeps completion-cache generation alive even when QA subcommand registration fails, and includes changelog evidence that the packaging/startup fix shipped after `2026.4.9`, so this issue is implemented on main.

## Evidence
