## Summary

The reported runaway `Tool WebSearch not found` loop is already covered by shipped OpenClaw safeguards: the unknown-tool stream guard now stays on by default, rewrites repeated missing-tool calls into plain text, and broader loop detection exposes a configurable global no-progress circuit breaker.
