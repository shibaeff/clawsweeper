## Summary

Current main, and the released v2026.4.23 tag, already implement the core behaviors requested here: internal gateway restarts are SIGUSR1-based rather than SIGTERM-based, restart emission is deferred until in-flight work drains (with a documented timeout), and startup now recovers pending outbound deliveries instead of leaving them orphaned after a restart window.

## Evidence
