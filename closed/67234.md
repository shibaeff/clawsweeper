## Summary

Current `main` already fixes the underlying MiniMax image-routing problem through the intended provider contract: M2.x chat models are kept text-only and the image tool routes MiniMax image work to `MiniMax-VL-01`. This makes the PR's `isMinimaxVlmModel()` expansion obsolete, and the proposed path would also bypass caller-supplied image timeouts because `minimaxUnderstandImage()` still hardcodes a 60s abort.

## Evidence
