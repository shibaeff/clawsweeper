## Summary

Current `main` already covers the reported TensorRT-LLM blocker through the generic custom OpenAI-compatible provider path, and it now strips the `store` field on proxy-style `/v1/chat/completions` routes, which matches the April 23 `extra_forbidden ... store` report.
