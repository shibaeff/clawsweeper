## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not include `openclaw-weixin` in speech-core's native voice-note routing, and the only linked fix attempt (#62331) was closed unmerged after its author noted it did not actually fix published Weixin delivery because the external plugin still ignored `audioAsVoice`. The report remains plausible and actionable, but the right fix likely needs validated plugin-side and/or contract-capability work rather than closing as already fixed.
