## Summary

Current main already separates restart from stop for the gateway: restart is routed through authorized `SIGUSR1`, while `SIGTERM` is stop-only. The launchd-supervised restart path now exits cleanly and explicitly avoids launchd kickstart/start handoffs, and restart requests are coalesced with a 30s cooldown. That directly addresses the reported 2026.3.24-era SIGTERM restart loop.
