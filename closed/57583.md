## Summary

Current `main` already fixes the reported heartbeat-label problem through broader session-origin handling: heartbeat/system-event turns do not write session origin/label state, and regression tests cover the non-synthesis/preservation behavior. This PR's `ConversationLabel` change is therefore superseded on `main`.

## Evidence

- **System-event origin writes are skipped on current main:** `deriveSessionMetaPatch` is called with `skipSystemEventOrigin: isSystemEvent` during session init, so heartbeat/cron/exec turns do not derive a new origin from the inbound context.
