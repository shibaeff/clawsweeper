## Summary

Current `main` routes config-read legacy-rule loading through Slack’s dedicated `doctor-contract-api` sidecar, which matches the stack in #63122 and avoids loading the broader Slack contract surface during `openclaw wiki init` / `openclaw wiki status`. The same implementation is present in release `v2026.4.23`.
