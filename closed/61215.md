## Summary

Current main already makes slug-generation timeout configurable through the existing `agents.defaults.timeoutSeconds` path, and that behavior is present in the released `v2026.4.23`, so this PR's separate `slugTimeoutMs` knob is redundant.

## Evidence

- **Slug generator now reads configurable timeout:** `resolveSlugGeneratorTimeoutMs` checks `cfg.agents?.defaults?.timeoutSeconds` and `generateSlugViaLLM` forwards the computed `timeoutMs` into `runEmbeddedPiAgent`, replacing the old hardcoded-only behavior.
